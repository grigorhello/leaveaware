Frequently asked questions

Is LeaveAware a mobile app?

Yes. LeaveAware is a mobile-first app available on iOS and Android. You can manage all team leave directly from your phone—request time off, approve requests, and view your team's availability anytime, anywhere.

How do push notifications work?

Managers get instant push notifications when new leave requests arrive. Employees are notified immediately when their requests are approved or rejected. No more waiting for emails—everything happens in real-time.

Can managers approve requests from their phone?

Absolutely. Managers can view pending requests and approve or reject them with a single tap, right from the notification or the app. Takes seconds—no forms, no complexity.

Do employees get notified when leave is approved or rejected?

Yes. Employees receive a push notification immediately when their request is approved or rejected. They also see the status in the app. Transparency and speed in one.

Does LeaveAware support public holidays?

Yes. LeaveAware automatically includes public holidays for your country. Holidays appear in the calendar so everyone knows when the team is off.
