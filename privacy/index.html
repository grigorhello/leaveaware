Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

LeaveAware (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the LeaveAware website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

When you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Email address (when joining the waitlist)
  • Team size or company size (optional)
  • Messages sent via live chat

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically:

  • Device type
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Session duration
  • Approximate geographic location
  • Referral source

2. Analytics & Tracking Tools

We use trusted third-party tools to understand how visitors use our website.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure website traffic and usage patterns.

Google Analytics may collect:

  • anonymized IP address
  • device and browser information
  • pages visited and time spent on pages

Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how users interact with our website through heatmaps and session recordings.

Clarity helps us improve usability and design.

Sensitive information is automatically masked.

Google Tag Manager

Google Tag Manager is used to manage and deploy analytics tools on our website. It does not collect personal data itself.

3. Email Waitlist

If you join our early access list, we collect your email address via Formspree.

Your email may be used to:

  • notify you when LeaveAware launches
  • provide product updates
  • provide early supporter discounts

We do not sell or share your email with third parties.

4. Live Chat

Our website uses Tawk.to for live chat support.

When you use chat, Tawk may collect:

  • your messages
  • device and browser information
  • optional contact details

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for:

  • analytics
  • website functionality
  • improving user experience

You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
