Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What’s included in LeaveAware Core?

LeaveAware Core includes unlimited employees, leave requests and approvals, a shared team calendar, leave balance tracking, notifications, and company holidays.

What extra features does Smart include?

LeaveAware Smart includes everything in Core, plus staffing alerts, conflict detection, team availability heatmaps, department coverage rules, manager insights, and priority support.

How does the 30-day free trial work?

Your team starts on LeaveAware Smart for 30 days at no cost. When the trial ends, you can stay on Smart or switch to Core.

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?

No. You can start your 30-day trial without entering a credit card.

Can I downgrade from Smart later?

Yes. You can switch plans anytime, and your team data stays intact.

Will my data be lost if I change plans?

No. Your employee data, leave history, and settings remain safe and accessible when you change plans.

Is LeaveAware hard to set up?

No. Most teams can get started in just a few minutes without complex setup or training.

Can employees and managers both use it?

Yes. Employees can request leave and view balances, while managers can review requests, track team availability, and prevent conflicts.

Can my team start small and grow later?

Yes. LeaveAware is designed for growing teams, so you can start with your current team and add more employees as you grow.

Who is LeaveAware best for?

LeaveAware is built for small teams that want a simple way to manage time off and stay aware of staffing coverage.
